The Pomodoro technique is a time management technique that breaks down a task into time intervals (typically 25 minutes), with short breaks (of 5 typically 5 minutes) 
in between them. 
On completion of 4 pomodoros, you take a long break (of typically 15-30 minutes).
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique
This application is a Pomodoro Timer that lets the user define his own time intervals and short breaks, and notifies the user as per his requirements.
